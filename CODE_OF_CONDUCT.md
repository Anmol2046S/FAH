# Code of Conduct\n\n## Our Pledge\n\nIn the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to making participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.\n\n## Our Standards\n\nExamples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:\n- Using welcoming and inclusive language\n- Being respectful of differing viewpoints\n- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully\n- Focusing on what is best for the community\n- Showing empathy towards others\n\nExamples of unacceptable behavior:\n- Harassment, offensive comments\n- Trolling, insulting comments\n- Sexual harassment or unwanted advances\n- Publishing private information without permission\n- Other unprofessional conduct\n\n## Enforcement\n\nInstances of abusive behavior may be reported by emacting conduct@example.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.\n\n---\n\n**Thank you for helping make FAH a welcoming community!**\n
